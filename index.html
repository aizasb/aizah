About

Nuraizah runs Madcat World, a startup focusing on mobile technology and lifestyle apps for communities. She is also the ASEAN Access Ambassador and Malaysia's local consultant to ASEAN in a 5-year project to enhance greater digital adoption amongst women entrepreneurs.

She began her career as a Product Engineer at Motorola Malaysia, working across Malaysia, Germany, and Scotland, earning two technical excellence awards and three productivity excellence awards. She then joined Sapura Nokia as an R&D engineer before founding CWorks Mobile in 2006 and Madcat World in 2010.

Nuraizah holds an undergraduate degree in Electrical Engineering from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, USA, and is currently pursuing a certificate in Data Science.
