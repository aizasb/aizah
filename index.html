About Aizah

Driven by a mission to help secure greater financial access for women, Nuraizah is a passionate advocate for the advancement of women entrepreneurs — inspired by each of the women she works with, and most closely by her mother, who raised her and her siblings as a single parent.

Nuraizah, fondly known as Aizah, is a Malaysia-based multi-award-winning technopreneur and serial entrepreneur. An Electrical Engineer by training, she began her career in engineering and R&D with Motorola and Nokia. She started her first company in 2006 and now runs Madcat World, a software development house, and MADCash, a fintech company focused on empowering women with micro businesses.

Nuraizah is also the ASEAN Access Ambassador and Malaysia's local consultant in a five-year project to encourage greater digital adoption among women entrepreneurs.
